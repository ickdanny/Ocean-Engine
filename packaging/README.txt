Controls

Menu Navigation
z = select
x = back
arrow keys = navigation

Gameplay
shift = focus
z = attack
x = bomb
arrow keys = movement
esc = pause
ctrl = skip dialogue

Left Handed Mode
fslash = shoot
period = bomb
wasd = movement

Developers Note

Greetings, esteemed player. It is I, THE AUTHOR.

Frankly, this has not an easy game to develop; I had expected six to be an auspicious number, but it has ended up being rather ill-fated instead. Nonetheless, I hope such issues do not affect your enjoyment of the game.

As I don't really have a speech or anything planned for this dev note, I figure I might as well share my philosophy of art with you all. Put simply, I believe that life is art. Well, I also believe that art encompasses even things outside the scope of life itself, but let's not go there today. What does it mean for life to be art? To be honest, I'm still figuring that part out (-w-;). But the importance of belief lies not in meaning but rather in action. What does life look like if it is conceptualized as art? Well, it probably looks something like Eucatastrophe.

See you next time.

ickdanny
